Apple Crisp

Description

Apple Crisp is a delicious dessert that features tender baked apples topped with a crispy, buttery oat mixture. This classic comfort food is perfect for fall gatherings or anytime you're craving something warm and sweet. It's much easier to make than apple pie but delivers all the same wonderful flavors.

Serve this Apple Crisp warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the ultimate dessert experience. The contrast between the warm spiced apples, crunchy topping, and cold ice cream creates an irresistible combination that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 6 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work best), peeled and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples with lemon juice and vanilla extract, tossing to coat.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together granulated sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Sprinkle over the apples and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Transfer the apple mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish, spreading them out evenly.
  5. In another bowl, combine oats, flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  6. Add the melted butter to the oat mixture and stir until crumbly.
  7. Sprinkle the oat topping evenly over the apples.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  9. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream if desired.
