Old Fashioned Gingerbread

Description

Old Fashioned Gingerbread is a timeless dessert that brings warmth and comfort with its rich molasses flavor and aromatic spices. This traditional recipe creates a moist, cake-like bread with the perfect balance of sweetness and spice that has been enjoyed for generations.

The deeply fragrant combination of ginger, cinnamon, and cloves makes this gingerbread perfect for holiday gatherings or cold winter evenings. Serve it warm with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a truly satisfying treat that will fill your home with the most wonderful aroma as it bakes.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup molasses
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup hot water

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch square pan.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and butter until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the egg, then stir in the molasses until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and salt.
  5. Gradually stir the dry ingredients into the molasses mixture.
  6. Add the hot water and stir until all ingredients are well combined.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature, optionally topped with whipped cream or a light dusting of powdered sugar.
